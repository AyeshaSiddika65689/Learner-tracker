PIE-CHART

A pie chart is a circular statistical graphic, which is divided into slices to illustrate numerical proportion.The arc length of each slice is proportional to the quantity it represents.


BAR GRAPH

It gives pictorial representation of data in the form of vertical or horizontal rectangular bars, where the length of bars are proportional to the measure of data.
